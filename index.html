About Clock-5

Clock-5 is a minimalist clock interface designed for InkCase, an E-ink iPhone case. To extend battery life, it refreshes only once every five minutes—trading precision for calm and restraint.

By shifting from exact time to a sense of time, it helps users stay in rhythm rather than in a rush. Clock-5 is a quiet intervention where design meets technology—solving power consumption through visual logic, and shaping behavior through form.

Clock-5 是为 InkCase 设计的一款极简时钟界面。为了延长电子墨水屏的续航，它每 5 分钟才刷新一次。少了分秒必争，多了从容与克制。

这款时钟以「时间感」取代「时间点」，让用户在五分钟的区间内掌握节奏，不焦虑，不急躁。Clock-5 是设计介入技术的一个注脚——用视觉逻辑解决电量问题，用形式语言影响行为习惯。
